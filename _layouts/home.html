Vote

Election Day is April 6 and mail-in ballots must be postmarked by election day. A ballot drop-off box will be available at the Los Feliz Branch Library at 1874 Hillhurst Ave between Friday, 4/2 and Tuesday, 4/6.

How to Vote

Any stakeholder who lives, works, owns property, or has a substantial and ongoing participation in a specific district may vote for a Los Feliz District Representative. All stakeholders in Los Feliz may vote for Community Interest Representatives.
